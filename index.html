Joy's Multi-Effect Guitar Pedal

A project for McGill University's MUMT306 class

What?

Hi, I'm Joy and I'm going to make my own multi-effect guitar pedal!

Why a Guitar Pedal?

  • I picked up the electric guitar somewhat recently and have been learning to play!
  • I get to create a piece of equipment I can actually use, and therefore personalize it to my needs...

Effects

  • Distortion
  • Reverb
  • Delay

Overview of Components

  • Input jack
  • Input stage
    • Audio amplifier
    • Capacitors: filter noise and stabilize signal
  • ADC
  • Distortion
    • Diodes: clip signal
    • Potentiometer
  • Reverb
    • DSP library
    • Low pass filter
    • Potentiometer
  • Delay
    • Resistor
    • Potentiometer
  • DAC
  • Output stage
    • Audio amplifier
    • Resistor
  • Output jack
  • footswitch
  • Battery / Power Adapter
  • 3 LEDs
  • 3 push buttons/toggle switches
  • External appearance

Foreseen Challenges

  • Audio quality
  • Noise and interference
  • Choosing power supply
  • Choosing the right components
  • testing
